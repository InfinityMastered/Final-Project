4th of July Celebration

On the 4th of July there will be fireworks at 8:00pm to 8:20pm. There will be special items in the gift shop such as small flags. All restuarants will have a 5% dicount on burgers ,fries and hotdogs.



Rules

Tampering with or interfering with the fireworks in any way will result in a fine of over $300 and being banned from all of the park locations.
You may not launch your own fireworks or set off firecrackers inside the park.
