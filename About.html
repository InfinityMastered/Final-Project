About Us


We started this park with a dream to give people the most enjoyable experience in the world. The park was bult in 2016 and in 2019 we got a recommendation from trip advisor that this was one
of the top Parks in the world. By 2020 we are hoping to have 25 rides and 2 locations. If you have any ideas for future attractions or rides you can contact us at 432-555-6329. We hope that everyone that visits our Park will enjoy the experience.

Q:Are we allowed to bring food and drinks inside the park?

A: No. All bags are searched at check in. No outside food and drinks are allowed except for water.



Q: How much do the tickets cost?

A: Be sure to check the tickets section on our website for that.



Q: How many rides do you have?

A: We have 18 rides in our park ready to be ridden on and enjoyed!



Q: What is your schedule and what days are you open?

A: We are open everyday from 9am to 11pm!



Q: Where do we go when we lose an item?

A: You will go to the Lost and Found which is located in the parks entrance.



Q: Are animals allowed in the park?

A: Only service animals are allowed in the park.
