Become the next great wizard as you explore the depths of the Wizardry World!

Enter the depths of the wizardry world through the castle of FogWarts. You will pass through iconic locations and become one with Hairy Pooter as you experience what he goes through on a day to day basis. For Hairy Pooter fans we will even let you get off the rides to explore the office of Dumbledork and get to meet your very own Dubby.

This ride will be crazy as you experience the spells casted on you, and your friends and family. You will be shocked at the outcome of your ride as you will see a real simulation of quidditch, and after the ride will get to try a special moon that soars you into the air! So make sure you don't miss this one and enjoy the ride!
